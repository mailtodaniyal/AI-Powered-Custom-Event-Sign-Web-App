Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our sign rental service

Is the sign a rental or purchase?
Our service is a rental. You pay for the design and rental period (typically 3 days). After your event, we either collect the sign or you can return it to us. This eco-friendly approach helps reduce waste and keeps costs affordable.
Can I upload my own photos?
Absolutely! Our AI designer allows you to upload photos and logos to incorporate into your sign design. The AI will seamlessly blend your images with the generated design for a personalized result.
How long is the rental period?
The standard rental period is 3 days, which covers setup, event day, and takedown. If you need the sign for a longer period, we offer extended rental options at checkout.
Do you deliver?
Yes, we offer delivery within a 25-mile radius of our location for a small fee. Outside that area, additional delivery charges may apply. You can also choose to pick up your sign from our studio at no cost.
